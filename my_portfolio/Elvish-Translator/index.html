About Tengwar

Tengwar is an alphabet created by J.R.R. Tolkien, devised in his mythology by the Elf Fëanor. It is used to write several of Tolkien's invented languages, most notably Quenya and Sindarin, as well as English and other real languages.

How this works

This translator phonetically maps English sounds to Tengwar characters using the "English mode." Digraphs like th, sh, and ng are mapped to single glyphs. For your visitors to see Elvish glyphs, install the Tengwar Annatar font.

Sindarin & Quenya

Use the mode tabs above to look up known Elvish words in Sindarin and Quenya — the two main Elvish languages Tolkien developed. Full grammar translation is beyond any automated tool as the languages were never completed.

Adding the font

Download Tengwar Annatar free, convert to .woff2, place it in your /fonts/ folder, and add a @font-face rule in your root CSS. The .ev-tengwar-text class will pick it up automatically.
